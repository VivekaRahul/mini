LIVING ROOM

The living room is one of the most social spaces in your home. Getting the decor right is the key to making a good first impression and setting the tone for the rest of the home.An elegant mix of two or three contrasting colours can create a cohesive design and a sense of symmetry. Begin with a base colour for the overall scheme, and add contrasting hues through furnishing and accessories. For a sense of opulence, experiment with shades of gold and silver.Choose a neutral or light colour for the living room walls and transform one wall into a focus wall. Instead of a solid colour, you can opt for stylish patterned wallpaper. You could also combine various textured surfaces, different furnishing materials or a bright accent piece of furniture that will stand out amidst the rest of the space.A carefully considered selection of shades, tones, and tints of the same colour can result in a spectacular design scheme. Remember to incorporate these in different aspects of your decor, such as surfaces, furniture, furnishing and accessories. Note: When white is added to the base colour it forms tints, while a darker grey or black can be added to produce shades and tones.If you love colours, then do not limit yourself to a restricted colour palette. Mix and match colours to create a vibrant decor by introducing a multi-hued carpet, throw cushions, curtains or colourful wallpaper. All these surfaces complement each other and are a perfect way to create a layered colour scheme.
